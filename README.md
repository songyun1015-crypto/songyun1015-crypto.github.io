## Research Interests
My research interests lie in arithmetic geometry. Specifically, I am interested in the cohomological Brauer group, unramified Brauer groups, the Bogomolov multiplier, and rational points on algebraic varieties.

---

## Master's Thesis
* **Title:** [The Local-Global Principle for Prescribed Norm Problem](https://tdr.lib.ntu.edu.tw/jspui/handle/123456789/102415)
* **Description:** This paper is a detailed survey of the appendix of the paper "<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.06024">Number fields with prescribed norms</a>". We reorganize the material in a self-contained way by presenting the necessary background knowledge and supplementing the technical details omitted in the original work.
